Common Questions from Mining Operations

Get answers to the most frequently asked questions

How does it handle 400-ton mining trucks?

Our stabilized roads are engineered specifically for heavy mining equipment. The chemical bonding process increases the California Bearing Ratio (CBR) by 300%, creating a load-bearing surface that actually gets stronger with traffic. We've successfully handled everything from 400-ton haul trucks to tracked excavators without any surface damage.

What happens in extreme rainfall (4,000mm+)?

Our technology creates an impermeable layer with 0.001% permeability - essentially waterproof. Even in areas with 4,000mm+ annual rainfall like parts of West Africa, our roads maintain structural integrity. The stabilized layer prevents water penetration that causes traditional road failures.

Can you work with our existing contractors?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive training to your existing contractors and can work as a technology partner. Our process uses standard construction equipment, and we provide detailed specifications and on-site training to ensure proper implementation.

How do you guarantee 15 years?

Our 15-year warranty is backed by extensive testing and 100+ successful projects. We provide performance bonds and have a proven track record. Our oldest installations from 2008 are still performing excellently, which gives us confidence in our warranty terms.

What about expansion joints and drainage?

Unlike rigid concrete, our stabilized roads have natural flexibility that eliminates the need for expansion joints. For drainage, we integrate with your existing systems and can improve water management by creating controlled runoff patterns.

Is it compatible with our environmental permits?

Yes, our technology is environmentally neutral and actually improves compliance. We reduce erosion, eliminate chemical runoff, and have approved Environmental Impact Assessments. Many clients use our roads to meet or exceed environmental requirements.
